About

BASE Advanced Excel Workshop

The Business Association of Supply Expertise(BASE) is a Rutgers Business School student organization focusing on preparing students for entering the world of supply chain. This program is created and ran by the Newark's BASE organization which offeres the certification for completion of the multi-week program and passing the organization administed exam. Student's participating in the program must complete BASE's Excel Level 1 program prior to joining the program. Anyone is welcome to access materials in the guides section of this site to prepare for working with Excel as well as Level One Excel content to help develop their skills, but will not be rewarded with certification unless they've completed the program.

Essential Data Skills

What You'll Learn

This advanced workshop builds upon your Excel foundation, equipping you with powerful tools for data analysis and manipulation. You'll dive into:

  • Basic Python for Data Analysis: Learn the fundamentals of data fetching in Python, a versatile language used extensively in data science.
  • Advanced Excel Data Cleaning: Discover techniques to efficiently clean and prepare complex datasets for analysis.
  • Power BI Visualization: Explore Power BI to create interactive dashboards and compelling data visualizations.
  • Excel Macros for Automation: Automate repetitive tasks and streamline your workflow with Excel macros.

Workshop Experience

In-Person Workshops

Engage in interactive, hands-on learning sessions led by experienced instructors. Our in-person workshops provide a collaborative environment to practice your skills and receive personalized guidance.

Rutgers Business School Certification

Upon successful completion of the Advanced Excel Workshop and passing the organization-administered exam, you'll receive a certification from the Rutgers Business School's Business Association of Supply Expertise (BASE). This certification validates your proficiency in advanced Excel, Python, Power BI, and data analysis skills, enhancing your professional portfolio.

Requirements

Prerequisites

To enroll in the Advanced Excel Workshop, participants must have successfully completed the BASE Excel Level 1 program. This ensures a foundational understanding of Excel basics, allowing us to delve into more advanced concepts.

Campus Availability

Please note that the in-person workshops are exclusively available on the Rutgers Newark campus.
